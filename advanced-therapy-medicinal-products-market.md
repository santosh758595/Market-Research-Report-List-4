<p><p>Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products (ATMPs) market includes various types such as cell therapy, CAR-T therapy, gene therapy, and tissue engineered products. Cell therapy involves the use of living cells to treat diseases, while CAR-T therapy involves modifying a patient's T-cells to target and kill cancer cells. Gene therapy aims to treat genetic disorders by correcting or replacing faulty genes. Tissue engineered products involve the manipulation of cells to develop functional tissues or organs for transplantation. These ATMPs offer promising treatment options for a wide range of diseases.</p></p>
