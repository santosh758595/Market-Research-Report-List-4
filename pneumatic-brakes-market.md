<p><p>Pneumatic brakes are used in various industries to control the speed and movement of rotating shafts or discs. Shaft pneumatic brakes are applied directly to the shaft to slow down or stop its rotation, while disc pneumatic brakes work similarly but are applied to discs for braking action. Both types of brakes use pneumatic pressure to engage and disengage the braking mechanism, providing reliable and efficient stopping power for different applications in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
