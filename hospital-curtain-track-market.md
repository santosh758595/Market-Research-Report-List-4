<p><p>Hospital curtain tracks are available in three different types: straight, 45 degree bend, and 90 degree bend. Straight curtain tracks are used for straight partitions, while 45 degree bend tracks are used for corners with a 45 degree angle. 90 degree bend tracks are specifically designed for corners with a 90 degree angle. These different types of curtain tracks cater to the specific needs of hospitals to create privacy and partitioning in various areas of the healthcare facility, providing versatility and flexibility in layout design.</p></p>
