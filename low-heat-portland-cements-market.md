<p><p>Low Heat Portland Cements are available in two main market types: Standard Consistency and Extra Thick Consistency. Standard Consistency cements have a moderate amount of plasticity and are commonly used in general construction projects. Extra Thick Consistency cements, on the other hand, have a higher plasticity and are often used in projects where high strength and durability are crucial, such as in bridges or high-rise buildings. Both market types offer a low heat generation during the hydration process, making them suitable for various construction applications.</p></p>
