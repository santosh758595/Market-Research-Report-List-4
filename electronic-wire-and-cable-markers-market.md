Printed adhesive cable markers are labels that adhere to the surface of the wire or cable. Plastic bar cable markers are plastic strips that slide onto the wire or cable for identification. Clip-on cable markers are markers that clip onto the wire or cable for easy installation. Electronic markers are markers that use electronic signals to identify and track wires and cables. Other markets include heat shrink markers, sleeve markers, and wrap-around markers.</p></p>
