<p><ul><li>Three-dimensional photonic crystals</li><li>Two-dimensional photonic crystals</li><li>One-dimensional photonic crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photonic Crystal Displays market consists of three main types: Three-dimensional photonic crystals, Two-dimensional photonic crystals, and One-dimensional photonic crystals. Three-dimensional photonic crystals are structures that exhibit a complete photonic band gap in all directions, providing unique optical properties. Two-dimensional photonic crystals consist of periodic nanostructures that manipulate light in two dimensions. One-dimensional photonic crystals are thin-film structures that control the flow of light in one direction. Each type offers distinct advantages and applications within the Photonic Crystal Displays market.</p></p>
