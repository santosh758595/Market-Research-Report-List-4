<p><strong>The Line Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Line isolators are electrical devices used to provide isolation between different sections of power systems to prevent faults and ensure system stability. The market for line isolators can be categorized into three main types based on their location in the system: Bus Side isolators are located at the connection point of multiple feeders in a substation, Line Side isolators are installed along transmission lines for maintenance purposes, and Transfer Bus Side isolators are used to isolate sections of a substation during maintenance or repairs. Each type serves a specific function in ensuring the reliability and safety of the power system.</p></p>
