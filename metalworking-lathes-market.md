<p><p>Metalworking lathes can be categorized into three main types based on their size and functionality: center lathes, engine lathes, and bench lathes. Center lathes are general-purpose machines used for moderate-sized workpieces. Engine lathes are larger and more robust, mainly used for heavy-duty industrial applications. Bench lathes are smaller and more compact, designed for use on a workbench or table. Each type caters to different needs and requirements within the metalworking industry, offering varying levels of precision, power, and versatility.</p></p>
