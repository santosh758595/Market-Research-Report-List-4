<p><p>Pad printing equipment market consists of two main types: single-color pad printers and multi-color pad printers. Single-color pad printers are used to print designs in only one color at a time, making them suitable for simpler designs and logos. Both types of pad printers cater to different printing needs and are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, electronics, and promotional products.</p></p>
