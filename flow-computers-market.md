<p><p>Flow computers are used in various industries for accurate measurement and control of flow in pipelines. Wired flow computers connect directly to the flow meters through physical cables, providing a reliable and stable connection. On the other hand, wireless flow computers use radio frequency technology to transmit data between the flow meters and the central control system, offering more flexibility and ease of installation. Both types of flow computers have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
