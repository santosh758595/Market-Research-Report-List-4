<p><strong>The High end Video Processor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QFN</li><li>TQFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High end video processors can be categorized into two main types: QFN (Quad Flat No-Lead) and TQFP (Thin Quad Flat Package) markets. QFN processors are known for their small size, low profile, and excellent thermal and electrical performance. On the other hand, TQFP processors are characterized by their thin flat package with leads extending from all four sides, making them suitable for high-density applications. Both markets cater to the high end video processing industry, providing advanced features and performance capabilities for demanding applications.</p></p>
