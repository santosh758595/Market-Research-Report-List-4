<p><p>Dental cone beam imaging, also known as cone beam computed tomography (CBCT), is a special type of x-ray technology used in dentistry to produce 3D images of the teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures. It is commonly used in oral surgery, orthodontics, and implant dentistry.</p><p>The dental cone beam imaging market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period.
