<p><strong>The Sailboat Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sailboat insurance can be categorized into two main market types: Actual Cash Value (ACV) and Agreed Amount Value. ACV insurance covers the current market value of the sailboat at the time of the loss, taking into account depreciation. On the other hand, Agreed Amount Value insurance allows boat owners to agree with the insurer on a specific value for the sailboat, which will be paid out in the event of a total loss. Each type of insurance offers different levels of protection and is suitable for different needs and budgets.</p></p>
