<p><p>There are two main types of Turbo Exhaust Valves market - Flange and Welded. Flange valves are designed to be bolted onto the exhaust system using a flange connection, making them easy to install and remove. Welded valves, on the other hand, are welded directly onto the exhaust system, providing a more secure and permanent connection. Both types offer benefits depending on the specific requirements of the application, such as ease of installation, durability, and resistance to leaks.</p></p>
