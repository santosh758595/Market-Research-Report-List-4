<p><p>Specialty PACS market typically offers two types of deployment options: On-Premise and Cloud. On-Premise PACS involves acquiring and maintaining the necessary hardware and software within an organization's own data center. This offers greater control and security but requires significant upfront investment. Cloud PACS, on the other hand, involves accessing the PACS software over the internet from a third-party provider's servers. This type of deployment is typically more cost-effective and scalable, making it popular among smaller healthcare facilities or those looking to reduce IT infrastructure costs.</p></p>
