<p><p>Diaphragm sealed manometers are pressure measuring devices widely used in various industries. The two main types of diaphragm sealed manometers are U Tube Manometers and Inclined Tube Manometers. U Tube Manometers consist of a U-shaped tube filled with liquid, where pressure changes cause the liquid levels to shift. Inclined Tube Manometers have a tube inclined at an angle, offering better visibility of readings. Both types are efficient in measuring pressure accurately and are commonly used in industrial applications.</p></p>
