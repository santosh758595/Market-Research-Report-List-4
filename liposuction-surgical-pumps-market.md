<p><p>Liposuction surgical pumps come in two main types: electric pumps and pneumatic pumps. Electric pumps utilize electrical power to generate pressure for the suction process, offering precise control and strength. On the other hand, pneumatic pumps use compressed air to create suction, providing a quieter and more portable option. Both types of pumps are commonly used in the liposuction procedure, with electric pumps being favored for their power and control, while pneumatic pumps are appreciated for their mobility and convenience.</p></p>
