They provide real-time glucose readings and alerts to help the user monitor and manage their blood sugar levels.</p><p>On the other hand, implantable CGM systems are inserted subcutaneously and provide continuous glucose monitoring without the need for frequent sensor changes. They offer convenience and accuracy for individuals looking for a more long-term solution for diabetes management.</p></p>
