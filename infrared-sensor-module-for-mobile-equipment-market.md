<p><p>Infrared Sensor Module for Mobile Equipment is categorized into two main types - Uncooled Infrared (UIR) Sensor and Cooled Infrared (CIR) Sensor. UIR sensors do not require cooling mechanisms and are commonly used in consumer electronics due to their cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, CIR sensors are equipped with cooling mechanisms to enhance their performance and are typically used in high-end applications such as military equipment and thermal imaging cameras. Both types serve different market needs with varying levels of performance and cost.</p></p>
