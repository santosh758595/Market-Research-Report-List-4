<p><p>Single Axis Solar Automatic Tracking System market involves systems that track the sun's movement on one axis (usually east-west). This system allows solar panels to continuously face the sun, maximizing energy production. On the other hand, Dual Axis Solar Automatic Tracking System market involves systems that track the sun's movement on two axes (east-west and north-south). This system offers even greater energy efficiency by accurately following the sun's path throughout the day, resulting in increased energy output compared to single axis systems.</p></p>
