<p><p>The Baggage Conveyor System market includes various types such as Delivery Conveyor, Injection Conveyor, Queue Conveyor, and Extended Queue Conveyor. Delivery Conveyors are designed to efficiently transport baggage from point to point within an airport terminal. Injection Conveyors help in diverting baggage onto different conveyor lines. Queue Conveyors are used to organize and control the flow of baggage at check-in counters. Extended Queue Conveyors are an extended version of Queue Conveyors, allowing for higher baggage capacity and increased efficiency in managing baggage flow.</p></p>
