Visible/near-Infrared (VNIR) utilizes wavelengths between 0.4 to 1.0 micrometers, Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) uses wavelengths between 1.0 to 3.0 micrometers, Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) covers wavelengths between 3.0 to 5.0 micrometers, and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) utilizes wavelengths between 8.0 to 15.0 micrometers. Each market type has its own applications and benefits in various industries such as environmental monitoring, agriculture, and defense.</p></p>
