<p><p>Medical corrugated tubing market consists of two main types: plastic corrugated tubing and metal corrugated tubing. It is commonly used in applications such as oxygen delivery, suction tubing, and IV sets. On the other hand, metal corrugated tubing is known for its high temperature resistance and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring high pressure and extreme temperatures, such as in surgical instruments and dental equipment.</p></p>
