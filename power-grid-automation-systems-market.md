<p><p>Power Grid Automation Systems market can be classified into two main types: On-Grid Automation Systems and Off-Grid Automation Systems. On-Grid Automation Systems are designed to automate and manage power distribution systems that are connected to the main utility grid. They ensure efficient control of power flow and help in reducing energy losses. On the other hand, Off-Grid Automation Systems are used in isolated locations where there is no connection to the main utility grid, providing reliable power supply through renewable sources such as solar or wind.</p></p>
