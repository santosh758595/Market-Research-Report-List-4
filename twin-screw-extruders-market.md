<p><p>Twin screw extruders are categorized into three main types: parallel co-rotating TSE, parallel counter-rotating TSE, and conical twin screw extruders. Parallel co-rotating TSE machines are widely used in the industry due to their high output rates and efficient mixing capabilities. Parallel counter-rotating TSE extruders are known for their excellent self-cleaning properties and gentle processing of materials. Conical twin screw extruders are preferred for applications that require high compaction and pressure, making them suitable for food and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
