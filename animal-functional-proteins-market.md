<p><p>Animal Functional Proteins Market includes three main types: hydrolysates, concentrates, and isolates. Hydrolysates are proteins that have been broken down into smaller peptides, making them easier to digest and absorb. Concentrates contain a higher amount of protein compared to other components in the product. Isolates are the purest form of protein, as they have been processed to remove most of the fats and carbohydrates, leaving mainly protein. Each type offers different benefits and applications in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
