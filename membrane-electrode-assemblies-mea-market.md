<p><p>Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEA) are essential components in fuel cells. There are three main types of MEA in the market: 3-layer, 5-layer, and 7-layer. The 3-layer MEA consists of a membrane sandwiched between two electrodes. The 5-layer MEA includes an additional gas diffusion layer on each side of the electrodes. The 7-layer MEA further adds catalyst layers to improve efficiency. Each type of MEA offers different benefits and applications, catering to various needs in the fuel cell industry.</p></p>
