<p><p>The Influenza Diagnostics Market is segmented into different types including RIDT (Rapid Influenza Diagnostic Tests), RT-PCR (Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction), Cell Culture, and Others. RIDT is a rapid diagnostic test that detects influenza antigens. RT-PCR is a molecular diagnostic test that detects influenza genetic material. Cell Culture involves growing the influenza virus in cells for identification. Other diagnostic methods include serology and immunofluorescence. Each type has its own advantages and limitations in diagnosing influenza infections.</p></p>
