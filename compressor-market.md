<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Compressor</li><li>Screw Compressor</li><li>Centrifugal Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressor market can be broadly classified into three types: reciprocating compressor, screw compressor, and centrifugal compressor. Reciprocating compressors use pistons to compress air, making them suitable for lower flow and higher pressure applications. Screw compressors use rotors to compress air, making them ideal for medium pressure air compression. Centrifugal compressors use a rotating impeller to create centrifugal force and compress air, making them suitable for higher flow and lower pressure applications. Each type of compressor caters to different needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
