<p><p>Diagnostic & Medical Imaging market consists of various types of imaging systems to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of medical conditions. X-ray Imaging Systems use electromagnetic radiation to create images of the internal structures of the body. Computed Tomography (CT) Scanners combine X-rays with computer technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images. Ultrasound Systems use high-frequency sound waves to create images of the body's organs and tissues. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Systems use magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images. Nuclear Imaging Systems use radioactive substances to create images of the body's functions. Other imaging systems include positron emission tomography (PET) scanners, mammography systems, and fluoroscopy systems.</p></p>
