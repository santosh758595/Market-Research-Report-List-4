<p><p>Digital X-ray devices market can be classified into two main types: Retrofit X-ray Systems and New Digital X-ray Systems. Retrofit systems are upgrades to existing traditional X-ray machines, allowing healthcare facilities to transition to digital technology without replacing their entire system. On the other hand, new digital X-ray systems are standalone machines that are designed with digital technology from the ground up. Both types offer high-quality imaging, increased efficiency, and improved patient care in healthcare settings.</p></p>
