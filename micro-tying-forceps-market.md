<p><p>Micro tying forceps are surgical instruments used in delicate procedures to hold and manipulate sutures. There are two main types of micro tying forceps on the market: straight tying forceps and curved tying forceps. Straight tying forceps have a straight design, making them ideal for accessing hard-to-reach areas without obstructing the surgeon's view. Curved tying forceps, on the other hand, have a curved design that allows for easier handling and manipulation of sutures in tight or angled spaces. Both types play crucial roles in securing sutures during surgical procedures.</p></p>
