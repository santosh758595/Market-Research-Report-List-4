<p><p>Dumpling Machines can be categorized into two main types: Full Automatic and Semi-automatic. Full Automatic Dumpling Machines are equipped with advanced technology that can automatically knead, fill, shape, and wrap dumplings with minimal human intervention. On the other hand, Semi-automatic Dumpling Machines require some manual input, such as placing the dough and filling ingredients, while the machine takes care of the wrapping process. Both types of machines cater to different production needs and are widely used in commercial kitchens and food processing industries.</p></p>
