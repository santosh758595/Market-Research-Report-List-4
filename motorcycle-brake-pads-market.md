Sintered Type brake pads are made by fusing metallic particles together under high heat and pressure, offering excellent durability and heat resistance. On the other hand, Ceramic Type brake pads are made from ceramic fibers and bonding agents, providing a smoother and more consistent braking performance. Both types cater to different rider preferences and riding conditions, offering a variety of options in the motorcycle brake pads market.</p></p>
