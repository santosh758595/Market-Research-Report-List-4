<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrospinning Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coaxial Electrospinning</li><li>Emulsion Electrospinning</li><li>Melt Electrospinning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrospinning machines are categorized into three types based on their working principle- Coaxial Electrospinning, Emulsion Electrospinning, and Melt Electrospinning. Coaxial Electrospinning involves the use of a core solution encased in a polymer sheath, producing fibers with specific properties. Emulsion Electrospinning uses two immiscible liquids as the spinning solution, leading to the formation of fibers with unique structures. Melt Electrospinning involves melting a polymer before spinning, resulting in uniform fibers with high mechanical strength. These different types cater to various applications and industries.</p></p>
