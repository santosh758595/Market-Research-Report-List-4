<p><p>Universal Life Insurance is a type of permanent life insurance that offers both a death benefit and a cash value component. The policyholder pays premiums into the policy, and a portion of the premium is invested by the insurance company, allowing the cash value to grow over time. This cash value can be accessed by the policyholder through loans or withdrawals, providing a level of flexibility not found in traditional life insurance policies.</p><p>The Universal Life Insurance market is expected to experience substantial growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period.
