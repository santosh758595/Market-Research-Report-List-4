<p><p>Land Freight Forwarding Market Types involve Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than Container Load (LCL) services. FCL refers to shipments that require a full container to transport goods from one location to another, while LCL involves shipments that do not fill an entire container, allowing multiple shippers to share space. FCL is typically more cost-effective for larger shipments, while LCL offers greater flexibility and cost savings for smaller shipments. Both services play a crucial role in the efficient movement of goods via land freight forwarding.</p></p>
