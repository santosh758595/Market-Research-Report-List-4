<p><strong>The Differential Pressure Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston</li><li>Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential pressure gauges can be categorized into two main types: piston and diaphragm. Piston differential pressure gauges use a piston mechanism to measure pressure differences between two points, providing high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, diaphragm differential pressure gauges use flexible diaphragms to measure pressure differentials, making them ideal for applications where vibration or pulsating pressures are present. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various industries such as HVAC, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p></p>
