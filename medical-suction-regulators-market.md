<p><p>Medical suction regulators are essential devices used to regulate the amount of suction pressure applied during medical procedures. There are two main types of suction regulators: continuous suction regulators and intermittent suction regulators. Continuous suction regulators provide a constant level of suction pressure, while intermittent suction regulators allow for periods of suction followed by intervals without suction. Both types of regulators are crucial in medical settings to safely and effectively remove fluids or other materials from a patient's body.</p></p>
