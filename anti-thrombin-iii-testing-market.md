<p><p>Anti-thrombin III testing market includes various types such as diagnosis of antithrombin deficiency, activity assays, and immunological assays. Diagnosis of antithrombin deficiency tests are used to determine the levels of anti-thrombin III in the blood, which helps in identifying deficiencies that can lead to increased risk of thrombosis. Activity assays measure the functional activity of anti-thrombin III in the blood, while immunological assays detect the presence of anti-thrombin III protein through antibody-antigen interactions. These tests play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing thrombotic disorders.</p></p>
