<p><p>Multi-turn valve market includes gate valves and globe valves, which are commonly used in various industries for controlling the flow of liquids and gases. Gate valves are designed to fully open or close with a linear motion, making them ideal for on/off service. Globe valves, on the other hand, offer more precise flow control due to their spherical shape and linear motion. Both types of valves are essential for regulating flow in pipelines and ensuring smooth operations in industrial processes.</p></p>
