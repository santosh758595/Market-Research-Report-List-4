<p><p>Metal polishing agents are used for removing imperfections on metal surfaces, bringing out a shiny finish. There are two main types of metal polishing agents on the market - paste and liquid. Paste agents are thick and abrasive, designed for removing deep scratches and restoring heavily tarnished surfaces. Liquid agents are smoother and less abrasive, mainly used for maintaining and polishing already clean surfaces. Both types are effective in achieving a polished finish, but the choice between paste and liquid depends on the condition of the metal surface and the desired result.</p></p>
