<p><ul><li>Proportional Reinsurance</li><li>Non-proportional Reinsurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine reinsurance market types include proportional and non-proportional reinsurance. Proportional reinsurance involves sharing risks and premiums between the ceding company and the reinsurer in a predetermined ratio. In contrast, non-proportional reinsurance provides coverage for specific risks or catastrophic events exceeding a certain threshold, with the reinsurer only paying out when losses surpass this limit. Both types play a crucial role in managing risk in the marine insurance industry by spreading exposure and ensuring financial stability for all parties involved.</p></p>
