<p><p>Sinusoidal output filters are categorized into different types based on their functionality and design. Sine wave output filters are used to smooth the output waveform and reduce harmonic distortion. 3-line output filters are designed for three-phase systems to improve power quality and reduce interference. LC sine wave output filters utilize inductors and capacitors to filter out harmonics effectively. dV/dT output filters are specifically designed to limit the rate of change of voltage over time, providing protection for sensitive equipment. Each type addresses specific needs in various applications.</p></p>
