<p><p>Residential air circulators come in two main types - wall-mounted and floorstanding/tabletop. Wall-mounted air circulators are installed on walls to save floor space and provide targeted air circulation. They are suitable for rooms with limited floor space. Floorstanding/tabletop air circulators, on the other hand, can be placed on the floor or a table for more flexibility in positioning. They are ideal for larger rooms where air circulation is needed. Both types of air circulators are designed to improve air quality and circulation in residential spaces.</p></p>
