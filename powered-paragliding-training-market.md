Quad training involves using a four-wheeled cart for take-off and landing, providing more stability and ease for beginners. Trike training involves using a three-wheeled cart with a motor for take-off and landing, offering comfort and convenience for those who prefer a simpler flying experience. Each market type caters to different preferences and skill levels within the powered paragliding community.</p></p>
