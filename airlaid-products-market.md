<p><p>Airlaid products are versatile materials used in various industries such as hygiene, medical, and industrial applications. There are four main types of airlaid products on the market: Latex Bonded Airlaid (LBAL), Thermal Bonded Airlaid (TBAL), Multi Bonded Airlaid (MBAL), and Hydrogen Bonded Airlaid (HBAL). LBAL utilizes latex as a bonding agent, TBAL uses heat and pressure for bonding, MBAL incorporates multiple bonding methods, and HBAL relies on hydrogen bonds between fibers. Each type offers unique properties and advantages for different applications.</p></p>
