<p><p>Brain implants market consists of various types such as Deep Brain Stimulation, Spinal Cord Stimulation, and Vagus Nerve Stimulation. Deep Brain Stimulation involves the implantation of electrodes in specific parts of the brain to treat neurological disorders. Spinal Cord Stimulation uses electrodes implanted along the spinal cord to alleviate chronic pain. Vagus Nerve Stimulation entails the placement of a device on the vagus nerve to regulate brain activity and treat conditions like epilepsy and depression. These technologies offer therapeutic options for patients with various neurological conditions.</p></p>
