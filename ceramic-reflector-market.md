<p><p>The Ceramic Reflector market can be categorized into two main types: Integral and Split. Integral reflectors have the ceramic material and reflective coating combined into one unit, providing a more compact and easy-to-install option. On the other hand, Split reflectors consist of separate ceramic material and reflective coating components, allowing for customization and flexibility in designing the reflector. Both types serve the purpose of reflecting light efficiently, but offer different advantages in terms of installation and customization.</p></p>
