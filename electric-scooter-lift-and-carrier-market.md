<p><p>The Electric Scooter Lift and Carrier market can be categorized into two main types: Interior and Exterior. Interior lifts are designed to be installed inside vehicles, typically vans or SUVs, providing a secure and convenient way to transport electric scooters. Exterior carriers, on the other hand, are mounted on the exterior of vehicles, often on the rear hitch, making it easier to load and unload scooters without taking up space inside the vehicle. Both types cater to different customer needs and preferences in the mobility aids market.</p></p>
