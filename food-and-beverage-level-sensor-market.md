<p><p>Food and beverage level sensors are essential for monitoring and maintaining liquid levels in various applications. Contact level sensors come into direct contact with the liquid to accurately measure its level. On the other hand, contact-less level sensors use technologies such as ultrasonic, radar, or infrared to measure the level without physical contact with the liquid. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and accurate monitoring of food and beverage levels in industries such as manufacturing, processing, and storage.</p></p>
