<p><p>Sandwich wall board, also known as SIPs (Structural Insulated Panels), is a type of construction material that consists of two outer layers of material (such as cement board, oriented strand board, or plywood) with a core of insulation material (such as expanded polystyrene foam, polyurethane foam, or mineral wool) sandwiched between them. This innovative building material offers excellent thermal insulation, structural strength, and moisture resistance, making it ideal for use in both residential and commercial construction projects.</p><p>The sandwich wall board market is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing demand for energy-efficient and sustainable building materials, growing construction activities in developing countries, and rising awareness about the benefits of using SIPs in construction.
