<p><p>Fluoride Ion Selective Electrodes are devices used to measure the concentration of fluoride ions in a solution. There are two main types of electrodes - Liquid Membrane and PVC Membrane. Liquid Membrane electrodes use an organic solvent to separate the ion-selective membrane from the sample solution, while PVC Membrane electrodes use a polymer membrane that is selective to fluoride ions. Both types of electrodes are widely used in various industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring for accurate fluoride ion measurements.</p></p>
