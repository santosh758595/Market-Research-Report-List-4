<p><p>Ultraviolet sensors are classified into three main types - UVA Sensor, UVB Sensor, and UVC Sensor. UVA sensors detect long-wave ultraviolet radiation, primarily responsible for tanning and skin aging. UVB sensors detect medium-wave ultraviolet radiation, which causes sunburn and skin cancer. UVC sensors detect short-wave ultraviolet radiation, typically used for disinfection purposes. Each type of sensor is designed to detect a specific range of ultraviolet light, catering to different applications such as skin protection, environmental monitoring, and sterilization.</p></p>
