<p><p>Tricuspid valve interventional therapy involves various methods to treat tricuspid valve disorders. Leaflet clamping is a technique where the valve leaflets are clamped together to improve valve function. Annulus retraction involves reducing the size of the valve's outer ring to enhance valve closure. Increasing the matching area refers to methods that optimize the contact point between the valve leaflets. These interventions aim to improve valve function and overall heart health in patients with tricuspid valve disorders.</p></p>
