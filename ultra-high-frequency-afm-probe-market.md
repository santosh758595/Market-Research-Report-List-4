<p><p>Ultra High Frequency AFM Probes are used in scanning probe microscopy for high-resolution imaging and analysis of nanoscale surfaces. Two common market types for these probes are Arrow Tip and Rotating Tip. </p><p>Arrow Tip probes have a sharp tip that allows for precise imaging of small features with high resolution. Rotating Tip probes have a tip that rotates during imaging, allowing for improved performance in non-contact mode. Both market types offer unique advantages for different applications in nanotechnology research and development.</p></p>
