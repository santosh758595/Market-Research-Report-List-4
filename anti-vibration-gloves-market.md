<p><p>Anti-vibration gloves are specialized safety gloves designed to reduce the effects of hand-arm vibration (HAV) syndrome, a condition caused by prolonged exposure to vibrations from power tools and machinery. This growth can be attributed to the increasing awareness of workplace safety, stringent regulations regarding hand-arm vibration exposure limits, and the rising adoption of anti-vibration gloves across various industries such as construction, manufacturing, mining, and healthcare.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the anti-vibration gloves market include the development of advanced materials for better vibration absorption, ergonomic designs for improved comfort and dexterity, and the integration of smart technologies for real-time monitoring of vibration exposure levels.
