<p><p>Rigid overhead catenary systems are used to support and electrify railway lines. Three main types of catenary systems are simple catenary, stitched catenary, and compound catenary.</p><p>Simple catenary systems consist of a single wire suspended from masts alongside the tracks. Stitched catenary systems include additional support wires to increase stability. Compound catenary systems involve multiple wires running parallel to each other to provide extra strength and resilience.</p><p>Each type of catenary system offers its own benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the railway project.</p></p>
