<p><p>Pressure-Volume Loop Systems market includes various types based on different cardiac parameters. Ejection Fraction monitors the efficiency of the heart in pumping out blood. Max and Min Ventricular dP/dt measure the rate of pressure change during contraction and relaxation. Cardiac Output indicates the volume of blood pumped by the heart per minute. Contractility Index reflects the heart's ability to contract. Elastance shows the heart's capacity to generate pressure. Stroke Volume represents the amount of blood pumped per heartbeat. Pre-load Recruitable Stroke Work measures the work done by the heart.</p></p>
