<p><p>Semiconductor package inspection systems are categorised into two main types based on their technology - optical based and infrared. Optical based package inspection systems use light to detect defects on semiconductor packages, offering high resolution imaging for accurate inspection. On the other hand, infrared package inspection systems use infrared light to identify flaws not easily visible to the naked eye. Both of these systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices before they are deployed in electronic products.</p></p>
