<p><p>The lower limb exoskeleton market can be categorized into two main types: powered and passive exoskeletons. Powered exoskeletons are equipped with motors that provide assistance to the user's movement, allowing for enhanced strength and mobility. On the other hand, passive exoskeletons do not have motors and instead rely on springs or other mechanical mechanisms to provide support and stability to the user. Both types of exoskeletons have their own set of advantages and are used in various applications such as rehabilitation, military, and industrial settings.</p></p>
