<p><p>Full body, upper body, and lower body are the three main types of soft exoskeleton markets. Full body exoskeletons cover the entire body and provide support for all body parts. Upper body exoskeletons focus on aiding the arms and upper torso, offering support and assistance for tasks that require upper body strength. Lower body exoskeletons concentrate on the legs and lower torso, enabling users to perform tasks that require lower body strength and mobility. Each type serves a specific purpose in improving movement and reducing strain on the body.</p></p>
