<p><p>Automotive auxiliary PTC heaters for electric vehicles come in three main types - Immersion Heaters, Radiator Heaters, and Pad Style Heaters. Immersion heaters are submerged in the coolant of the vehicle and directly heat it, while radiator heaters are installed within the vehicle's heating system. Pad style heaters are thin, flexible heating elements placed in specific areas of the vehicle to provide targeted heat. Each type of heater serves a unique purpose in efficiently heating electric vehicles and ensuring passenger comfort.</p></p>
