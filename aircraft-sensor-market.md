<p><p>Aircraft sensor market can be classified into different types based on their functionalities. Pressure sensors are used to measure air pressure in different parts of the aircraft. Position sensors determine the exact location of various components in the aircraft. Force sensors measure the force applied on different parts of the aircraft. Temperature sensors monitor the temperature inside and outside the aircraft. Vibration sensors detect vibrations that can affect the aircraft's performance. Radar sensors are used for detecting and tracking objects in the surrounding airspace. Other sensors may include proximity sensors, flow sensors, and level sensors.</p></p>
