<p><p>Body composition analyzer scales are available in various types, including bio-impedance analyzers, dual-energy x-ray absorptiometry, air displacement plethysmography, and others. Bio-impedance analyzers measure the body's resistance to electrical currents, dual-energy x-ray absorptiometry uses x-rays to measure bone density and body composition, and air displacement plethysmography measures body volume using air displacement. Other types of body composition analyzers may include infrared interactance and hydrostatic weighing. Each of these methods offers unique benefits and applications for assessing an individual's body composition.</p></p>
