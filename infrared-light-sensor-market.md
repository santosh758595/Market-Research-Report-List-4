<p><p>In the Infrared Light Sensor market, you can find two main types of sensors: Contact Light Sensors and Non-Contact Light Sensors. Contact Light Sensors require physical contact with the object being measured, while Non-Contact Light Sensors can sense light without direct contact. Contact Light Sensors are often used for precision measurements, while Non-Contact Light Sensors are preferred for applications where physical contact is not possible or desired, such as in industrial automation or consumer electronics.</p></p>
