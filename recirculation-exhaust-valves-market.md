<p><strong>The Recirculation Exhaust Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Pressure Signal</li><li>Single Pressure Signal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Recirculation exhaust valves in the market are available in two main types: Dual Pressure Signal and Single Pressure Signal. Dual Pressure Signal valves are designed to respond to both high and low pressure signals, allowing for more precise control over the recirculation of exhaust gases. On the other hand, Single Pressure Signal valves only respond to a single pressure signal, making them simpler and more cost-effective but providing less flexibility in terms of control. Customers can choose the type of valve that best suits their specific needs and budget.</p></p>
