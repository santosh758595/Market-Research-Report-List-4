<p><p>Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVD) are used in ophthalmic surgeries to protect the cornea and maintain space within the eye. Dispersive OVDs spread out over the eye's surface, cohesive OVDs maintain their shape and do not spread, combination OVDs have properties of both dispersive and cohesive OVDs, and visco-adaptive OVDs adapt their viscosity based on the surgical condition. Each type of OVD offers distinct benefits for different surgical procedures, catering to the diverse needs of ophthalmic surgeons.</p></p>
