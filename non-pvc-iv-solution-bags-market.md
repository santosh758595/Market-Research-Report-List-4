<p><p>Non PVC IV solution bags are available in two main types: single-chambered and multi-chambered. Single-chambered bags are designed for containing a single type of IV solution, such as saline or glucose. These bags are commonly used for basic fluid administration. On the other hand, multi-chambered bags have separate compartments for different solutions, allowing for the mixing of medications or additives. These bags are used for more complex medication administration and are particularly useful in hospital settings for delivering tailored treatments to patients.</p></p>
