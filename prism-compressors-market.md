<p><p>Prismatic pulse compressors are a type of compressor that uses a single prism to compress light pulses, resulting in a more efficient compression process. On the other hand, multiple-prism pulse compressors consist of multiple prisms arranged in a series to achieve even higher compression ratios. Both types of compressors are used in various applications such as ultrafast optics and laser systems, where precise compression of light pulses is required for improving performance and efficiency.</p></p>
