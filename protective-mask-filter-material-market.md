<p><p>Protective mask filter materials are classified into two primary types: general purpose and medical grade. General purpose filter materials are typically made from non-woven fabrics and are suitable for everyday use in non-medical settings. On the other hand, medical grade filter materials are designed to meet specific regulatory standards for use in medical settings to provide higher levels of filtration and protection against pathogens. These materials are usually made from specialized materials such as melt-blown polypropylene to ensure effectiveness against viruses and bacteria.</p></p>
