<p><ul><li>Differential Type</li><li>Relative Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic pressure sensors are classified into differential type and relative type based on their sensing mechanisms. Differential type sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, providing precise readings for applications where pressure variations are critical. On the other hand, relative type sensors measure pressure in relation to atmospheric pressure, making them suitable for applications where the absolute pressure is of importance. Both sensor types play a crucial role in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
