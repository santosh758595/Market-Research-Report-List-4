<p><strong>The Semiconductor Dry Etching System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Reaction</li><li>Physical Removal</li><li>Combination of Chemical Reaction & Physical Removal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor dry etching systems come in three main types: chemical reaction, physical removal, and a combination of both. In chemical reaction type etching, the semiconductor material is dissolved through a chemical reaction with a gas or plasma. Physical removal involves physically sputtering or scraping away the material. The combination type utilizes both chemical reactions and physical removal to achieve the desired etching results. Each type offers unique advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
