<p><p>Data as a Service (DaaS) is offered through three main market types - Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud DaaS involves data storage and processing via a third-party service provider on a shared infrastructure. Private Cloud DaaS offers data services on a dedicated cloud environment, ensuring higher levels of security and control. Hybrid Cloud DaaS combines elements of both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of both while balancing cost and security considerations.</p></p>
