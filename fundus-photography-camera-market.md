<p><p>Fundus photography cameras are used to capture images of the back of the eye for diagnostic purposes. There are different types of fundus photography cameras on the market, including OCT (optical coherence tomography) and slit lamps. OCT cameras use light waves to create detailed cross-sectional images of the retina, while slit lamps combine a microscope with a high-intensity light source to examine the structures at the back of the eye. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions.</p></p>
