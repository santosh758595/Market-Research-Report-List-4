<p><ul><li>Meltblown</li><li>Spunbonded</li><li>Spunlace</li><li>Needle Punch</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-woven medical textiles market types include meltblown, spunbonded, spunlace, needle punch, and others. Meltblown textiles are made by blowing melted fibers onto a surface. Spunbonded textiles are created by extruding fibers into a web. Spunlace textiles are produced by entangling fibers using high-pressure water jets. Needle punch textiles are made by punching fibers mechanically. Other types of non-woven medical textiles include thermal-bonded and chemical-bonded fabrics. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits for different medical applications.</p></p>
