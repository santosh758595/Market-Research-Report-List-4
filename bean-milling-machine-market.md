<p><p>Bean milling machines are available in two main market types: vertical machines and horizontal machines. Vertical machines are designed to grind beans vertically, with the beans falling through the machine and exiting at the bottom. Horizontal machines, on the other hand, grind beans horizontally, with the beans passing through the machine and exiting on the same level. Both types of machines have their own advantages and are used in various industries for processing beans efficiently.</p></p>
