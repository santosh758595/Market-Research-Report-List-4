There are two main types of leadless pacemaker systems: single-chambered systems and double-chambered systems. Single-chambered systems have only one pacing lead that is connected directly to the heart, while double-chambered systems have two pacing leads for more precise control of the heart's electrical signals. Both types offer significant benefits in terms of flexibility, reduced risk of complications, and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
