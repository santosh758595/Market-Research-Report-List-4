<p><p>Elliptical fitness machines come in three main types: rear-drive, front-drive, and center-drive. Rear-drive machines have the drive system located at the back of the machine, providing a smooth and natural elliptical motion. Front-drive machines have the drive system located at the front, offering a more compact design and efficient use of space. Center-drive machines have the drive system placed in the middle, providing a balance between the other two types in terms of size and motion. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of users.</p></p>
