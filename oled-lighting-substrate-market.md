Metal substrates offer better thermal management and structural stability, making them ideal for larger area lighting fixtures. Fabric substrates are soft, lightweight, and can be easily integrated into textiles, making them suitable for creating innovative and customizable lighting solutions. Each substrate type offers unique benefits for different lighting applications.</p></p>
