<p><p>Single Drum Vibratory Roller is available in two main types: Walking type and Driving type. Walking type rollers are manually propelled by an operator walking behind the machine, making them more suitable for smaller projects and tight spaces. On the other hand, Driving type rollers are equipped with a seat for the operator to drive the machine, offering greater efficiency and control for larger construction sites. Both types are commonly used in road construction, landscaping, and other projects requiring compaction of soil or asphalt.</p></p>
