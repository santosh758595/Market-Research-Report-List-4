<p><p>Drop-on-demand Inkjet Printers are divided into three main market types: Thermal Inkjet Printers, Piezo Inkjet Printers, and Electrostatic Inkjet Printers. Thermal Inkjet Printers use heat to create bubbles that eject ink droplets onto the paper. Piezo Inkjet Printers use piezoelectric crystals to push out ink droplets. Electrostatic Inkjet Printers use an electric field to move charged ink droplets onto the paper. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different printing needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
