<p><p>GMO test kits are used to detect genetically modified organisms in food and agricultural products. There are two main types of GMO test kits on the market: PCR and ELISA. PCR test kits use polymerase chain reaction to amplify and detect specific DNA sequences, while ELISA test kits use enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays to detect proteins. Both types of test kits are widely used in the food industry to ensure compliance with GMO labeling regulations and to verify the presence of GMOs in products.</p></p>
