<p><p>Viral testing market types include various techniques such as Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) Test, Immunochromatographic Assay, RT-PCR-Based Test, Agglutination Assay, Flow-Through Assay, and Solid-Phase Assay. DFA test utilizes fluorescent antibodies to detect viral antigens, while Immunochromatographic Assay detects specific viral proteins. RT-PCR-Based Test amplifies and detects viral genetic material, Agglutination Assay detects viral particles through clumping, Flow-Through Assay utilizes flow-through technology, and Solid-Phase Assay detects viral antigens using solid surfaces. These techniques cater to different needs and preferences in viral testing.</p></p>
