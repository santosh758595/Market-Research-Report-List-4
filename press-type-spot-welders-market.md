<p><p>Press type spot welders are available in three main market types: manual welding machines, semi-automatic welding machines, and automatic welding machines. </p><p>Manual welding machines require the operator to manually load and unload the parts to be welded, as well as to initiate the welding process. </p><p>Semi-automatic welding machines partially automate the welding process, with the operator loading the parts but the machine controlling the welding cycle. </p><p>Automatic welding machines are fully automated, with the machine taking care of all welding process steps without manual intervention.</p></p>
