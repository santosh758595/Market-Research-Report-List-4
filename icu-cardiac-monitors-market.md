<p><p>ICU Cardiac Monitors are devices used in intensive care units to monitor the heart's activity and detect abnormalities. There are two main types of ICU Cardiac Monitors: Implantable Cardiac Monitors, which are inserted under the skin to continuously monitor heart rhythms; and Conventional Cardiac Monitors, which are external devices used for short-term monitoring. Implantable Cardiac Monitors provide continuous monitoring for long periods, while Conventional Cardiac Monitors are used for shorter-term monitoring in critical care settings. Both types play a crucial role in managing cardiac patients in the ICU.</p></p>
