<p><p>Radiation detection materials and equipment market consists of various types of detectors, including gas-filled detectors, scintillation detectors, and solid-state detectors. Gas-filled detectors use gases such as helium or argon to detect radiation. Scintillator detectors use materials like sodium iodide or cesium iodide to convert radiation into light. Solid-state detectors utilize semiconductors like silicon or germanium to detect radiation. Each type of detector has its advantages and applications in different fields such as healthcare, security, and nuclear power.</p></p>
