<p><p>A thermal transfer printer is a type of printer that uses heat to transfer ink onto paper, labels, or other materials. This process involves a printhead applying heat to a ribbon that then transfers the ink onto the material being printed on. These printers are commonly used for applications that require high-quality and long-lasting printing, such as barcodes, labels, and tags.</p><p>The global market for thermal transfer printers is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for efficient printing solutions in industries such as manufacturing, retail, healthcare, and logistics.
