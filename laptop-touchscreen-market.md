<p><p>The laptop touchscreen market is segmented into four main types: Resistive Touchscreen, Capacitive Touchscreen, Surface Acoustic Wave Touchscreen, and Infrared Touchscreen. Resistive touchscreens work by pressing two layers together to create a touch point. Capacitive touchscreens use the electrical charge of the human body to detect touch. Surface Acoustic Wave touchscreens use ultrasonic waves to detect touch. Infrared touchscreens use infrared light beams to detect touch. Each type has its own unique technology and advantages.</p></p>
