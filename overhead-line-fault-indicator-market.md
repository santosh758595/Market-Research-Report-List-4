<p><strong>The Overhead Line Fault Indicator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pole-mounted Indicator</li><li>Conductor-mounted Indicator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Overhead Line Fault Indicators can be categorized into two main types: Pole-mounted Indicators and Conductor-mounted Indicators. Pole-mounted Indicators are installed on the utility poles along the overhead lines, providing a visual indication of any faults occurring in the line. On the other hand, Conductor-mounted Indicators are directly attached to the conductors of the overhead lines, offering a more direct and immediate detection of faults. Both types of indicators play a crucial role in improving the reliability and efficiency of overhead power distribution systems.</p></p>
