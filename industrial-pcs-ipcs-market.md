<p><p>Industrial PCs (IPCs) come in different market types based on their cooling systems. Fanless Industrial Computers are designed without any internal cooling fans, making them quieter and more reliable in harsh environments with dust or other particles. Fanned Industrial Computers, on the other hand, use internal fans for cooling which allows for better heat dissipation and higher processing power. Both types cater to different needs in industrial settings, with fanless computers being more suitable for rugged conditions and fanned computers for higher performance requirements.</p></p>
