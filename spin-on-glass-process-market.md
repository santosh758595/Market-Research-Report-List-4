<p><strong>The Spin-on Glass Process Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-Temperature Spin Process</li><li>Normal Spin Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spin-on Glass (SOG) process is a method used to deposit a thin, uniform layer of glass onto a substrate by spinning a liquid glass precursor. There are two main types of SOG processes: Hot-Temperature Spin Process and Normal Spin Process. </p><p>Hot-Temperature Spin Process involves spinning the glass precursor at high temperatures to achieve improved adhesion and densification of the glass film. This process is typically used for advanced applications requiring high-quality glass films.</p><p>Normal Spin Process, on the other hand, is conducted at room temperature and is suitable for more standard applications where high adhesion and densification are not critical.</p></p>
