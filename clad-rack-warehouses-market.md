<p><p>Clad rack warehouses typically come in two market types - pallet rack type and cantilever rack type. Pallet rack type warehouses utilize shelving units designed to store palletized goods, maximizing vertical space within the warehouse. Cantilever rack type warehouses are designed for storing long and bulky items such as lumber or pipes, with arms extending from vertical columns to support the load. Both types offer efficient storage solutions for different types of inventory, catering to diverse warehouse needs.</p></p>
