<p><p>Access cards market can be broadly classified into three types -Proximity Cards, Smart Cards, and Others. Proximity cards are contactless cards that use radio frequency identification technology for access control. Smart cards, on the other hand, have an embedded microprocessor that can store and process data, making them more secure than proximity cards. The Others category includes biometric access cards, magnetic stripe cards, and key fobs. Each type offers varying levels of security, convenience, and compatibility with existing systems.</p></p>
