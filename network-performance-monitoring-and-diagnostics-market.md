<p><p>Network Performance Monitoring and Diagnostics market types include Device Polling Technology, Flow-Based Technology, and Packet-Based Technology. Device Polling Technology involves collecting data from network devices at regular intervals to assess performance. Flow-Based Technology analyzes network traffic patterns to identify issues. Packet-Based Technology captures and inspects individual packets of data for in-depth analysis. Each type offers different approaches to monitoring and diagnosing network performance, allowing organizations to choose the best solution based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
