<p><p>The Visible Fingerprint Lock market includes two main types: Optical Fingerprint Lock and Semiconductor Fingerprint Lock. Optical fingerprint locks use light and sensors to capture an image of the fingerprint and verify the identity of the user. Semiconductor fingerprint locks use a small silicon chip to store the fingerprint data and compare it with the scanned fingerprint. Both types offer secure and convenient access control solutions for residential and commercial properties, with semiconductor locks generally providing higher levels of security and reliability.</p></p>
