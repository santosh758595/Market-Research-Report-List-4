<p><p>Needleless syringe market includes various types such as jet injectors, inhaler technology, transdermal patch, and novel needle-free technologies. Jet injectors deliver medication through a high-pressure stream without a needle. Inhaler technology allows medication to be delivered directly to the lungs. Transdermal patches deliver medication through the skin. Novel needle-free technologies use methods like microneedles or pressurized gas to deliver medication without a needle. Each type offers unique advantages for patients who prefer to avoid traditional needle injections.</p></p>
