<p><p>Smart positioner market types can be categorized based on type and technology. By type, they are classified as single acting positioners and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners control the position of a control valve by regulating the air pressure on one side of the actuator. Double acting positioners control the position of a control valve by regulating the air pressure on both sides of the actuator. By technology, smart positioners can be mechanical, electro-pneumatic, or smart/digital, offering varying levels of precision and control.</p></p>
