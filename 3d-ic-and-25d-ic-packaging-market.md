<p><strong>The 3D IC and 25D IC Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3D Wafer-level Chip-scale Packaging</li><li>3D TSV</li><li>2.5D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D IC packaging involves stacking multiple integrated circuits on top of each other, increasing performance and reducing footprint. 3D wafer-level chip-scale packaging involves stacking and connecting dies on a wafer before dicing. 3D TSV packaging uses through-silicon vias to connect stacked dies vertically. The 2.5D market involves using interposers to connect multiple dies on a single substrate, offering increased performance at a lower cost compared to full 3D IC packaging. These packaging types aim to meet the increasing demand for smaller, more powerful electronic devices.</p></p>
