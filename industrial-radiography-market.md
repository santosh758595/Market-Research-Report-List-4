<p><p>Industrial radiography is a non-destructive testing method used in various industries for inspecting the integrity of materials and components. In digital radiography, images are captured electronically and can be viewed and analyzed immediately, offering faster results and easier storage and sharing. In film-based radiography, images are captured on traditional X-ray film, which requires developing before analysis. Both digital and film-based radiography have their own advantages and are utilized depending on specific industry requirements.</p></p>
