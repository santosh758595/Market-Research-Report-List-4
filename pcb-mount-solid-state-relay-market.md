<p><p>PCB Mount Solid State Relays (SSRs) are available in three main types - AC Output SSRs, DC Output SSRs, and AC/DC Output SSRs. AC Output SSRs are designed for controlling AC loads, while DC Output SSRs are used for DC load control. AC/DC Output SSRs are versatile and can be used to control both AC and DC loads. Each type of SSR offers unique benefits and applications, making them suitable for various industries and electronic devices requiring precise and reliable switching capabilities.</p></p>
