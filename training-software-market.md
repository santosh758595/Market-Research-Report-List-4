<p><p>Training software is offered in two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based training software is accessed through the internet and is typically maintained by the vendor, allowing for easy scalability and remote access. On the other hand, on-premises training software is installed and run on local servers within an organization's own infrastructure. While on-premises software may offer more control over data security and customization, cloud-based software is often more flexible and cost-effective due to the subscription-based pricing model.</p></p>
