<p><strong>The Beer Enzymes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amylase</li><li>Protease</li><li>Glucanase</li><li>Cellulase</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beer enzymes market can be categorized into five types based on their functions: amylase, protease, glucanase, cellulase, and other enzymes. Amylase enzymes break down starch into sugars, while protease enzymes break down proteins. Glucanase enzymes target beta-glucans, which can cause haziness in beer. Cellulase enzymes break down cellulose for improved filtration. Other enzymes encompass a variety of functions including lipase for better foam stability. Each type of enzyme plays a crucial role in the brewing process to enhance the quality and characteristics of beer.</p></p>
