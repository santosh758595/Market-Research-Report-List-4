<p><p>Marine & Offshore Cranes market can be categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Fixed Boom Cranes are stationary and provide strong lifting capabilities. Telescopic Boom Cranes have extendable arms for increased reach. Knuckle Boom Cranes have flexible joints that allow for better maneuverability in confined spaces. Knuckle-telescopic Cranes combine the features of both telescopic and knuckle boom cranes. Other types of cranes include specialized designs catering to specific industry requirements such as pipe handling or offshore wind farm installations.</p></p>
