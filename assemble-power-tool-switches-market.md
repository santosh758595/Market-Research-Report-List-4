<p><p>The power tool switches market can be categorized into various types based on their functionality. These types include DC and AC speed control switches, single speed switches, micro switches, trigger switches, rocker switches, and others. DC and AC speed control switches allow users to adjust the speed of the power tool. Single speed switches offer a fixed speed setting. Micro switches are small and sensitive, triggering the tool's operation. Trigger switches are handheld switches that control on/off functionality. Rocker switches have a simple on/off design. Others include specialty switches for specific power tools.</p></p>
