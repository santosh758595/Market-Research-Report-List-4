<p><p>Demister market types include wire-mesh demisters, baffle-type demisters, and others. Wire-mesh demisters use layers of interwoven wire to trap liquid droplets, while baffle-type demisters use plates or baffles to redirect droplets and allow gas to pass through. Other demister types may include fiber-bed demisters or vane-type demisters. Each type offers different benefits and is suited for specific applications in industries such as petrochemical, chemical, and oil & gas.</p></p>
