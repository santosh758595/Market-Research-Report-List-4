<p><p>Rapid Thermal Processing Equipment Market includes two main types: Lamp-based and Laser-based. Lamp-based RTP equipment uses high-intensity lamps to heat the wafer material quickly and uniformly. On the other hand, Laser-based RTP equipment utilizes lasers to provide precise control over the heating process. Both types offer advantages such as improved energy efficiency, faster processing times, and reduced thermal budget. The choice between lamp-based and laser-based equipment depends on specific application requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
