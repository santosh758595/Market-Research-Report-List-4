<p><p>UV Printing market refers to the use of ultraviolet ink to print on various materials, including ceramics. This type of printing offers high-quality, durable prints that are resistant to fading and scratching. Dye-sublimation Printing market, on the other hand, involves transferring dye onto ceramic surfaces through a heat press. This method produces vibrant, long-lasting prints with excellent color accuracy. Both UV Printing and Dye-sublimation Printing are popular choices for ceramic printing due to their effectiveness and versatility.</p></p>
