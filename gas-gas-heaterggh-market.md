<p><p>Gas Gas Heater (GGH) market is divided into three main types: Regenerative Type, Rotary Type, and Other markets. The Regenerative Type GGH uses a regenerative heat exchanger to preheat the combustion air, resulting in improved energy efficiency. The Rotary Type GGH features a rotating heat exchanger to transfer heat between the flue gas and combustion air. The Other market includes various types of GGHs that do not fall into the regenerative or rotary categories, offering different benefits and applications for users.</p></p>
