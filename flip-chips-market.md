<p><p>Flip chips, also known as controlled collapse chip connection or direct chip attach, are a type of integrated circuit packaging technology where the semiconductor device is attached face-down onto a substrate, typically using solder bumps. This design offers several advantages such as shorter signal paths, higher input/output density, and better thermal performance compared to traditional wire bonding methods.</p><p>The Flip Chips Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.20% during the forecast period. Additionally, the adoption of flip chip technology in various applications like smartphones, tablets, automotive electronics, and industrial machinery is further fueling market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the flip chips market is the growing adoption of 2.5D and 3D flip chip packaging technologies.
