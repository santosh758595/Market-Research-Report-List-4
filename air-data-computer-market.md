<p><strong>The Air Data Computer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog Air Data Computers use continuously variable signals to represent data, while Digital Air Data Computers use discrete signals, typically in binary form. Analog systems are simple, cost-effective, and favored in older aircraft, while digital systems offer higher precision, faster processing speeds, and better compatibility with modern avionics. Both types serve the same purpose of collecting, processing, and transmitting air data information to assist in flight operations, but digital systems are becoming more prevalent in the market due to their advanced capabilities.</p></p>
