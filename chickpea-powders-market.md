<p><p>Chickpea powder is classified into two main market types: Desi and Kabuli. Desi chickpea powder is made from small, dark brown chickpeas and is commonly used in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisines. It has a nutty flavor and is higher in fiber and antioxidants than Kabuli chickpea powder. Kabuli chickpea powder, made from larger, cream-colored chickpeas, is milder in flavor and commonly used in Western cuisines. It is known for its smooth texture and higher protein content compared to Desi chickpea powder.</p></p>
